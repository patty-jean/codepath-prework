Marvelous Multichromatic Musical Memory Match

Welcome to the Marvelous Multichromatic Memory Match! The rules are simple: The buttons will blink and play a sound to you and you have to play the buttons back in the same order in which they were played to you. You can either click then with your mouse or tap the number key on your keyboard corresponding to the number on the button.

Let's get groovy!
